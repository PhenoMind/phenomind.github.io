The Challenge

Mental health care faces critical gaps that prevent optimal patient outcomes

Care is data‑blind

Mental health management is episodic — brief visits and long gaps — leaving clinicians without continuous, objective data.

Scale vs demand

1B+ people live with mental health conditions; only 1 in 4 adults receives treatment and 50% never reach a specialist.

Subjective endpoints

Drug validation often relies on self‑report, delaying insight into efficacy and adverse events.
